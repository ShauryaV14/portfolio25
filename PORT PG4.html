ESSVEE PRESS:

Campaign Design

EssVee Press is a printing company dedicated to reviving the art of traditional printing. Specializing in crafting using a vintage printing press, EssVee Press combines time-honored techniques with modern design sensibilities to produce unique and high-quality printed materials.

The company offers immersive workshops that invite participants to experience the magic of vintage printing firsthand. EssVee Press is committed to preserving the rich heritage of print while inspiring a new generation of creators through their meticulously crafted products and engaging educational experiences.

The campaign design project for EssVee Press aimed to promote this hypothetical company and their vintage printing press through a blend of workshop event posters, billboard advertisements, and social media posts. With the slogan "From Press to Impress: Inspiring Brilliance," the campaign utilized eye-catching visuals.

The strategic placement of posters and billboards ensured maximum visibility and helped drive interest and participation in the workshops. This integrated approach effectively highlighted EssVee Press's unique offerings, combining tradition with creativity to engage and attract their target audience.
